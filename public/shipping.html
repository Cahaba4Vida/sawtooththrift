Shipping

Sawtooth Thrift primarily fulfills orders via shipping. In limited cases, local pickup may be offered by arrangement.

Processing time

Orders ship every Saturday. If you order after Saturday, your item will ship the following Saturday unless the listing states otherwise.

Shipping rates

Shipping cost (if any) is shown on the product listing and/or during checkout. We may offer free shipping on select items.

Local pickup (optional)

If a product is marked Pickup only or offers local pickup, instructions will be provided after purchase or by message.
